General view:
* 4 organisers are planning a jazz and soul music festival in Sète Village.

Location and musicians:
* The location will be Sète hilltop, with a panoramic view on the sea and a perfect backdrop for the music.
* The jazz pianist Marcus Williams and the vocalist Sarah Collins will be invited to perform on stage.

Stage:
* A vintage-inspired scene with modern lighting effects is chosen.
* Giant saxophones and venyls will decorate the stage.

External contractors:
* Experimented sound engineers with top sound equipment will be recruited.
* A professional security team will be enrolled to keep the entrances and patrol on the festival grounds.
* Local vendors will sell from seafood to soul food.
