François Hollande and Nicolas Sarkozy discuss foreign policy, specifically the situation in Afghanistan and the decision to withdraw French troops. François Hollande believes that the decision to send troops was made by his predecessor, Lionel Jospin and Jacques Chirac in 2001, and that he has not changed the decision. Nicolas Sarkozy defends the decision to withdraw troops, stating that it was a joint decision made by him and other European countries, and that it was necessary due to the terrorist threat in the region. François Hollande also discusses the situation in France, including the recent terrorist attack in Toulouse and the need for stricter gun control laws.
