Real Reaction's television remote control development team, including Andrew, Kendra (User Interface Designer), Kate, and Katie (Industrial Designers), holds its initial meeting to outline project objectives. The team aims to create an innovative, stylish, and user-friendly remote control with profitability in mind. They emphasize collaborative brainstorming using a whiteboard and encourage creative contributions, reflecting their commitment to a unique design. The discussion focuses on user-friendly aspects such as well-labeled buttons and ergonomic use, with the goal of creating a remote control that works effectively from any angle. The team also considers the option of marketing the remote control as both stylish and practical, exploring the possibility of interchangeable face plates and novel features to appeal to a wide audience. The meeting sets the stage for further detailed design discussions in pursuit of their goal.
