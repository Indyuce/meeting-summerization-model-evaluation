During the meeting, the focus is on conceptualizing the design of a remote control. Discussions revolve around key aspects such as component design, energy source, case material, buttons, and circuit board. The team weighs the feasibility of incorporating touch screen technology and voice recognition, considering their in-house capabilities and potential manufacturing costs. The desire to create a unique and environmentally friendly product is prominent. Additionally, marketing trends that emphasize innovation and technological advancement are considered. The consensus leans towards using voice recognition due to its existing development and simpler implementation. The team also contemplates various casing options and aims to target the youth market with a non-traditional design, incorporating customizable cases and color options. The importance of cost-effectiveness, prototype development, and adherence to coaches' instructions is underscored.
