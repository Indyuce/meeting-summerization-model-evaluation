The meeting focuses on the functional design of a remote control for a TV, involving individual presentations, agenda sharing, and minute-taking. The goal is to determine the target group and functions, with each presentation highlighting an energy source, user interface, and infrared terminal. The emphasis is on simplicity, clarity, and small size. Another discussion revolves around the design of a remote control, addressing the incorporation of additional buttons, user behavior, and appearance. Voice recognition's appeal to the 15-25 age group is noted, along with the possibility of voice-based channel changes.
