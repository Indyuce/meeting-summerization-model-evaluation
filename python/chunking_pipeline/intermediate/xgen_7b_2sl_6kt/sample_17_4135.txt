The meeting is about creating a new remote control for a TV. The project aims to create a remote control that is original, trendy, and user-friendly. The team has divided up into three parts: functional design, individual work, and detailed design. The functional design will be done by the Industrial Designer, individual work by the User Interface Designer, and detailed design by the Industrial Designer. The team will introduce themselves, share their favourite animals, and draw their favourite animal. The meeting will also discuss project goals, tool training, and project plan.
