Linagora is an open-source software development company founded over twenty years ago. Its founders are French, and use sovereignty as a commercial argument. Indeed, open-source software prevents malicious use of data. Their main product is the LintO voice assistant, once a research project that has now been industrialized. It accounts for 40% of the company's sales, if we do not mention the support activity. The stake nowadays is to keep being as competitive as possible, in a market dominated by big private companies such as Google or Meta. The director of Linagora announced a million investment in this tool, which is a breakthrough for this tool. It claimed that its performances will exceed all me know, and shape the future of AI. Public opinion is really enthusiastic to see what comes next.
